Summary of Changes
Section Previous Language Updated Language
1 - Indications Indicated for moderate-to-severe plaque psoriasis in adults Indicated for moderate-to-severe plaque psoriasis in adults and pediatric patients aged 12 years and older
2 - Dosage 300 mg SC at Weeks 0, 4, then Q8W Adults: 300 mg SC at Weeks 0, 4, then Q8W
Pediatric (>=60kg): 300 mg; (<60kg): 150 mg
5 - Warnings Infections, hypersensitivity reactions Infections, hypersensitivity reactions, hepatotoxicity (new), suicidal ideation monitoring (new)
8 - Specific Pops No pediatric data available Pediatric safety/efficacy established in patients >=12 yrs based on SHIELD-PEDS trial

Key Highlights

The most significant update is the expansion of the indication to include pediatric patients aged 12 and older, supported by data from the SHIELD-PEDS Phase III trial (n=285). Additionally, two new warnings have been added based on post-marketing surveillance.
