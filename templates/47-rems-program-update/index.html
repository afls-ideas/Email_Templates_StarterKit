This communication provides important updates to the NeuroCalm REMS (Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategy) program. All prescribers and dispensing pharmacies must comply with the updated requirements by the deadline noted above.
Updated REMS Requirements
1

Prescriber Certification

Complete updated online training module and knowledge assessment (est. 45 min). Re-certification required for all previously certified prescribers.
2

Patient Enrollment

All patients must be enrolled in the REMS program before first prescription. Updated enrollment form (v4.0) now requires documented pregnancy testing protocol.
3

Pharmacy Certification

Dispensing pharmacies must recertify with updated dispensing protocols. Only certified pharmacies may dispense NeuroCalm.
Patient Monitoring Requirements
• Baseline CBC, LFTs, and pregnancy test (females of reproductive potential)
• Monthly CBC and LFTs for first 6 months, then quarterly
• Monthly pregnancy tests for females of reproductive potential
• Neuropsychiatric assessment at baseline, Month 3, and Month 6
• Patient-reported outcomes survey at each office visit
Reporting Obligations
Prescribers must submit quarterly REMS compliance surveys and report any serious adverse events within 15 calendar days. Failure to submit reports may result in suspension of prescribing privileges under the REMS program.
