Efficacy and Safety of ImmunoGuard (imurelizumab) in Moderate-to-Severe Rheumatoid Arthritis: Results of the SHIELD-RA Phase III Trial
The New England Journal of Medicine • Vol. 392, No. 8 • pp. 714-728
Published online: February 2026 • DOI: 10.1056/NEJMoa2612345
Dear Dr. {{recipient.lastname}},
I'm pleased to share a recently published study that may be of interest given your work in rheumatology. The SHIELD-RA trial provides new Phase III data on ImmunoGuard (imurelizumab).
Key Findings
ACR50 Response: 61.2% of patients on ImmunoGuard vs. 32.1% on placebo at Week 24 (p<0.001)
DAS28-CRP Remission: 38.7% achieved remission (DAS28-CRP <2.6) at Week 24
Radiographic Progression: Significant inhibition of structural damage (mTSS change 0.3 vs 1.8, p<0.001)
Safety Profile: Consistent with known profile. Serious infection rate: 2.1% vs. 1.8% (placebo)
